Glucose Patch Microstrip Antenna
Assisted th Dr. Omidvar research to develop optimal impedance and current distribution material for RF transmission in MATLAB and Desmos. Engineered a patch microstrip antenna component for custom PCB with an alumina dielectric biosensor. Calculated theoretical MIP (molecular imprinting technology) stub resonantor frequency at target 24GHz required for increasing glucose sensitivity, improving the non-invasive biosensors reliability.
Turbo Electric Nano Generator
Fabricated a Triboelectric Nanogenerator (TENG) to output electrical energy for components requiring 5V. Optimized particle kinetic energy capture mechanism with a 6-blade turbine by 3D-printing with PLA composite. Achieved a conversion output of 0.7V at 38Hz by utilizing the electro-negativity difference between Copper and PTFE.
Custom Force Syncope-prevention Device
Developed a prolonged syncope-prevention device using a 4.5V solenoid actuator, PulseSensor, MOSFET Regulator and ESP32. Inspired by a friend's concussions symptoms this device prevents prolonged unconscious’s for individuals when alone. With the heart rate sensor when the Delta of the readings drastically chnage the acctuator applies a force in the center of the chest aiming to increase blood preasure.

Traffic Simulation Optimizer
I am deeply passionate about optimizing efficiency and innovating within public transit and civil infrastructure. I conducted a simulation of a typical 4-way intersection to determine optimal cycle times, incorporating variables such as traffic flow rates and driver behavior. I wrote a brief paper analyzing the constraints and trade-offs involved in intersection design, including cost, efficiency, and user dynamics.
CDR Stock Visualizer
When CIBC introduced Canadian Depository Receipt, it was hard to visualize gains, fees and opportunity cost when investing in weaking Canadian dollar against the US Dollar. I built this visualizer in streamlit supported by Python, to retrieve Y-Finance data and plot the relative growth rate where users can replicate different situations with various broker fees, currency exchange rates, and growth timelines to understand when an optimal time to exist and enter positions. Using Plotly-Express, this compiled multiple calculators into one used for US equites only traded both in the NASDAQ/NYSE and the Canadian NEO Exchange.
